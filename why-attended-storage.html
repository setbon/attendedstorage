Why attended storage ?

The idea of a reliable staffed storage comes out of the frustration of not being able to find one in Manhattan. After weeks of search, the only found alternatives consist of overpriced do-it-yourself storage, a limited number of valet spaces or package rooms that one can only access by being a resident or guest of the building. In short, a complete lack of a space in downtown or midtown Manhattan where one could leave a case or package long term or even short term for that matter where they will hold the key for you or simply place your case in a shared space where no key is required when you want to send a representative to pick it up or return it. The matter becomes even more challenging if dealing with an oversized transport or flight case.

In industries such as live events and entertainment where last minute bookings are common, to guarantee service it is simply essential to have access to essential equipment close to the venues where events or shows are happening. Having the capability to retrieve an item last minute without having to spend 4 hours going back and forth to your New Jersey warehouse can be of immense value.
